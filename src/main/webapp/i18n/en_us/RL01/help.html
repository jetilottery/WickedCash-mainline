Wicked Cash

Tiptoe through the creepy night as you search for Cauldrons and ‘Wicked Cash’ instant win prizes. Reveal 3 or more bubbling Cauldron symbols and win a prize! The more Cauldrons you reveal, the bigger the prizes. And if you can hold your nerve, you might unearth the Hat symbol for an instant win!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each $ symbol to reveal a Cauldron, a Cat or an instant win Hat symbol.

Reveal three or more Cauldron symbols to win a prize. The more Cauldron symbols revealed, the bigger the prize.

Reveal a Hat symbol to win a prize instantly.

When all symbols are revealed, the game ends. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
